The Dragon Newspaper Logo

From freshman to senior year, Nicolas has worked for The Dragon Newspaper, Design Tech High School's official student-run newspaper. He worked as an illustrator all four years, and wrote several articles during that time, including "In Person d.Lab" (p. 3-4). Along with being an artist, Nicolas also took on the leadership role of Director of Art & Photography for the newspaper team both his junior and senior year, and on the Web & Print Design team his senior year.
