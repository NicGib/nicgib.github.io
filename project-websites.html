During high school Nicolas found himself making websites for 2 different clubs. One made from scratch for the Game and Animation Club (AKA G/A Club), and the other for Design Tech High School's official athletics program, d.athletics, which was made alongside a team of club members.

Game and Animation Club

Variations of the Game & Animation Club logo.

The home page of the Game and Animation (AKA G/A club) website.

For the landing page, Nicolas' main design inspiration came from using gradients and organic shapes to make the first thing you see feel unique. He also designed the club's logo and the digital illustration found on the homepage.

d.athletics

The home page of the d.athletics website.

This website for the d.athletics team was made with a few other team members on Wix. It was easier to collaborate on and create something that looked good on Wix, but it doesn't allow for as much control than doing it from scratch. The picture shown above is the site's landing page.


From top-left to bottom-right, we have the site's footer design, sports photo gallery, club sports info (currently, this is limited to sailing), and d.athletics merch store advertisement/announcement.
